INSPIRATIONAL OBJECTIVES OF THE FOUNDER: MR.VALENTINE NDI

The organization aims to assist the education and health development of children and youths, with special emphasis on those who are educationally at risk including those who have been abused or neglected. Its work contributes to the body of knowledge and influences training and practice to positively affect educational outcomes of these intelligent but financially deprived children.
